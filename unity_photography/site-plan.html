Overview

Purpose

Have you ever thought about a special past moment in your life but it was quite blurry or you just can't fully picture it again? Have you ever wanted to share a special moment with somebody else but just can't explain it or help them imagine it? Don't worry, it is not just you. This is where my business comes in. Our purpose at Unity Photography is to capture those precious moments of your life for you to savour later on down the line. A picture is so much more than just colour. It is a moment in time to be cherished forever.

Audience

We welcome all who wish to capture their special moments. We are here to unite you with your cherished moments forever. We focus on special occasions such as weddings, holidays, family photos, baby showers and baby photos etc. The site will mostly work with adults who wish to have photos taken of them or with their loved ones. The site will be accessed mostly by desktop but will be open to any device.

Page 2 - Our services What we have to offer:
Weddings:
wedding day is one of the most special days in your life. It is a moment that should be kept forever. Choose us at Unity Photography to capture this amazing day for you. We produce only the best and absolutely beautiful photos that are works of art. We are precise and easy to work with. All you need to worry about is having the best time ever while we ensure that those memories will last forever.
Family:
Your family is so important, that's why it is vital for you to have photos of them. Family photos connect the past to the present. They hold stories and help us reflect on times past. Having family photos also helps preserve those in it for future generations to love and cherish. It allows you to look back on your life and the lives of your loved ones and stir up stories of years gone by.
School/Graduation:
Life always goes by so quickly and many forget their youth or “good years”. Whether you are graduating or are just taking a normal school photo, it is just so precious to capture your youth and school years. The time in your life will just fly by. School photos will show you how much you have grown. They will remind you of the amazing stories and fun times you had with friends. They will show you a different era/phase in your life.

Page 3 - FAQ Frequently asked questions
What is the pricing or how much does it cost to have a photoshoot? The pricing scales with the time we are hired to photoshoot and the number or size of the photographs you wish to have produced. On average a wedding is $2000-$3000, for family photos it is $200-$300 and for school or graduation photos it is typically $100-$150. All prices vary according to customers needs but we always offer the best prices. Photos do come in different combos.
How can I book a photoshoot? To book a photoshoot you can email us directly at UnityPhotography@gmail.com or phone our secretary with the number 0794928616. We will then inquire you about pricing, dates, times, number of photos and sizes of photos.
How long will it take to receive my photos? It depends on the number and size but usually take about 1 to 2 working weeks after the photoshoot. Galleries will be hand delivered to you directly at your desired location.
Where are we based and what area do we cover? We are base in East London, South Africa and normally operate within a 50-60-mile radius of the city but can travel further if needed.
