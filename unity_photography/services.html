What we have to offer

Family

Your family is so important, that's why it is so vital for you to have photos of them. Family photos connect the past to the present. They hold stories and help us reflect on times past. Having family photos also helps preserve those in it for future generations to love and cherish. It allows you to look back on your life and the lives of your loved ones and stir up stories of years gone by.

Wedding

Your wedding day is one of the most special days in your life. It is a new chapter in your life. It is a moment that should be kept forever. Choose us at Unity Photography to capture this amazing day for you. We produce only the best and absolutely beautiful photos that are works of art. We are precise and easy to work with. All you need to do is have the best time ever while we work our magic and ensure that those memories will last forever.

School

Life always goes by so quickly and many forget their youth or “good years”. Whether you are graduating or are just taking a normal school photo, it is just so precious to capture your youth and school years. The time in your life will just fly by. School photos will show you how much you have grown. They will remind you of the amazing stories and fun times you had with friends. They will show you a different era/phase in your life.
